Discount on motopay

There are some categories of business in India in which the shopkeeper has to give discount to his customers to maintain the business. Indian market is a market where consumers are accustomed to discounts. Due to which many times a situation of conflict is created between the shopkeeper and the customers due to which the relationship between the shopkeeper and the customers gets spoiled due to which it becomes difficult to run the business smoothly. Keeping this problem in mind, motopay has designed a platform so that both customers and shopkeepers do not have to worry about discounts. Under this system, motopay has fixed the discount percentage according to the category of the shop. And this system is very easy, simple and user friendly. To adopt this system, the shopkeeper only has to register his shop as a vendor in the Motopay platform. As soon as the shopkeeper registers, a unique QR code is generated for that shopkeeper. The discount percentage given by the shopkeeper to his customers is written in this QR code. Now when any customer SCAN & PAY in this QR code, the cashback (discount) without asking for it comes immediately in MOTOPAY WALLET which the customer can use again or can also WITHDRAWAL in his bank. In this way, MOTOPAY solves the discount issue between the shopkeeper and the customers, which improves the relationship between the customers and the shopkeeper and the chances of customers returning to that shop again increases up to 90%. Due to which the shopkeeper's business increases.

Referral Program

Motopay operates a referral program that prevents motopay users/members from leaving the motopay platform. A user can refer unlimited users and earn unlimited commission from their transactions. 2) The user/member is also given the right by motopay to add any shopkeeper with motopay. When any user connects any shopkeeper with his referral code, then that shop now becomes the shop of that user because now the user starts getting commission from every transaction of that shop. In this way, no user connected to motopay leaves the motopay platform and at the same time he purchases or avails other services from the same shop where the motopay QR code is installed. In this way, the chances of vendors associated with MOTOPAY getting permanent customers increases significantly.
