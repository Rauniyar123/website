About Motopay

Motopay is a venture established to tap into the growing possibilities of the E-commerce sector in India. Our primary objective is to provide consumers and members with daily use consumer products and services with guaranteed cashback at their nearest store. Motopay Private Limited, incorporated in the year 2023 and headquartered in Bhilai Durg, Chhattisgarh, is committed to achieving a significant position in the field of E-commerce with verious features. Motopay emphasizes core values such as quality of service, social responsibility, and an integrated structure. Our focus on Quality & Growth, Positive Initiative, Dedication, and Commitment towards work reflects our dedication to achieving predictable results. We uphold these values in our business relationships with suppliers and ensure not to compromise the interests of our customers. The pillars of Motopay's establishment include creating awareness for a better lifestyle, contributing to a better future through efficient operations, exploring new business equations for cost reduction, and increasing the institute's density year by year for wider community benefits.

On specific pages of the Platform, we employ data collection tools like "cookies" or similar technologies to analyze our webpage flow, gauge promotional effectiveness, and foster trust and safety. "Cookies" are small files positioned on your device's hard drive or storage that aid us in delivering our services. Importantly, cookies do not contain any of your Personal Information. Certain features are exclusively accessible through the use of a "cookie" or similar technologies. Moreover, cookies enable you to enter your password less frequently during a session and help us furnish information tailored to your interests. Most cookies are categorized as "session cookies," automatically erased from your device's hard drive or storage at the end of a session.

You retain the freedom to decline or delete our cookies or similar technologies if your browser or device allows. However, doing so might limit your access to specific features on the Platform, necessitating more frequent password re-entry during a session. Additionally, you may encounter "cookies" or similar technologies on certain Platform pages placed by third parties. It's important to note that we do not have control over the utilization of cookies by third parties.

Upon completion of the specified retention period, the Personal Information will be deleted in accordance with applicable laws.
