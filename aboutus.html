About us

Motopay Solutions Private Limited is a pioneering online platform facilitating seamless transactions between vendors and members for the procurement of goods and services. Our innovative approach bridges the gap between online convenience and offline shopping experiences.

Our Services:

At Motopay, members have the opportunity to explore a network of registered vendors, primarily physical stores, where they can directly purchase goods and services. Upon making purchases through our registered vendors, members enjoy exclusive benefits such as cashback or discounts, enhancing their shopping experience.

Payment Process:

Members conveniently make payments for their purchases through our secure online portal, with the transaction amount credited to their Motopay account. Subsequently, we transfer the payment to the respective vendors within a mutually agreed timeframe, typically within one day from the transaction date. While we strive for promptness, any delays attributable to payment gateway banks or other entities are beyond our control.

Core Operations:

Motopay operates as a facilitator, connecting customers with vendors offering a diverse range of products and services. We do not engage in the direct sale or provision of these offerings. Additionally, we extend our services to include online shopping and other services, as well as home services like plunber, electrician etc. facilities.

Location and Expansion

Headquartered in Bhilai, Chhattisgarh, Motopay Solutions Private Limited harbors ambitions of nationwide expansion. Our overarching aim is to establish a robust presence across India, fostering greater accessibility to our innovative platform and services. At Motopay, we are committed to revolutionizing the shopping experience, empowering both vendors and members alike with unparalleled convenience and value.
