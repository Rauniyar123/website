About Us

Motopay E-commerce sector in India.

Motopay Solutions Private Limited is a pioneering online platform facilitating seamless transactions between vendors and members for the procurement of goods and services. Our innovative approach bridges the gap between online convenience and offline shopping experiences. At Motopay, members have the opportunity to explore a network of registered vendors, primarily physical stores, where they can directly purchase goods and services. Upon making purchases through our registered vendors, members enjoy exclusive benefits such as cashback or discounts, enhancing their shopping experience.

Guranteed Cashback

"India's First E-commerce APP where customers can get guaranteed cashback without any negotiation by shopping at their Nearest Store."
Fast, Secure and User friendly Platform. Transact, Socialize & Earn. Scan & Pay made easy.

Monthly Shopping

In today's Era of Inflation, the monthly expenses of every family have increased a lot and it is becoming very difficult to manage. Keeping these things in mind, Motopay is trying to provide guaranteed cashback to its customers on every purchase so that customers can get some relief.

Scan QR Code

Since the Corona global pandemic, digital transactions have increased very rapidly, 80% of the transactions are happening through digital payments. Therefore, motopay also provides a unique QR code for all its business transactions, especially to the vendors associated with it, so that motopay members can make the payment for their shopping digitally and securely.

Cashback Rewards

Cashback for the motopay platform is not just a promotional activity that is limited to just a few days. We have developed a system whereby motopay members/users will get lifetime cashback on every shopping payment, guaranteed

Refer your friends

Motopay has also tried to give a new direction to the Refer and Earn program. We have not limited it to any limit. Motopay members can earn unlimited commission by referring unlimited members using their referral code and getting paid for all their services. Similarly, we provide another facility to our members that they can earn unlimited commission on every transaction by linking their nearest shop with Motopay. Thus, we provide all the members associated with us an opportunity to earn cashback as well as increase their income by using the platform.
